Tito Science is what happens when middle-aged chaos meets musical ambition. Born from jam sessions, late-night ideas, and way too much caffeine, we mix rock, funk, and a bit of weirdness—because why not? We're not here to chase trends, just good vibes, loud sounds, and the occasional existential crises in odd time.
